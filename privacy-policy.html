01

Who We Are

VeelIQ ("VeelIQ", "we", "our", or "us") is the company operating this website and the forthcoming VeelIQ platform — a Skills-as-a-Service product that provides plug-and-play AI skills and agents for individuals and businesses.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal data we collect through this website, including our early-access waitlist.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the waitlist and improve the product. This includes:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you sign up for early access.
  • Referrer URL — the page you visited immediately before arriving on our site, captured automatically by your browser.
  • User agent string — your browser and operating system information, collected automatically to understand our audience.
  • Sign-up timestamp — the date and time your submission was recorded.
We do not collect your name, phone number, payment details, or any sensitive personal data at this stage of the product. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising networks.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To add you to the VeelIQ early-access waitlist and notify you when access becomes available.
  • To send you launch-related updates that you have explicitly opted into by submitting the form.
  • To understand where our audience is coming from and improve the product experience.
  • To prevent duplicate registrations and maintain the integrity of the waitlist.

We will never sell your data or use it for purposes incompatible with those listed above.

04

Data Storage & Security

Waitlist data is stored in Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL platform with industry-standard security practices, including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Supabase infrastructure is hosted on AWS and is SOC 2 Type II compliant. Data is stored in the EU (Frankfurt) region.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100 % secure. We encourage you to use a unique email address when signing up.

05

Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database provider (see Section 4).
  • Google Fonts — font assets are loaded from fonts.googleapis.com. Google may log the request; see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal authorities — if required by applicable law or a valid legal process.

All third-party providers are contractually bound to process your data only as directed by us and in accordance with applicable data-protection laws.

06

Cookies & Tracking

This website currently does not use cookies, local storage, analytics scripts, or any persistent client-side tracking technologies. No consent banner is required at this time.

Should we introduce cookies or analytics in the future, this policy will be updated and appropriate consent mechanisms will be implemented before any tracking begins.

07

Data Retention

We retain waitlist data for as long as it is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected — specifically, to notify you when VeelIQ launches and to provide you with early access.

Once the product has launched and the waitlist is closed, or upon your request for deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the EU/EEA under GDPR, the UK under UK GDPR, and California under CCPA), you may have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of communications at any time by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@veeliq.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

09

Children's Privacy

VeelIQ is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify waitlist subscribers by email.

Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
